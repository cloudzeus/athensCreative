## About — "What is Athens Creative English?"

Key quotes:

> Children walk in and are instantly inspired. They breathe the air of
> freedom — colours, sounds.

> Student-led learning (vs. teacher-led learning) allows children to fully
> explore their creativity.

> Everyone learns to speak before they write! How will we know what to write
> if we don't know what to say?

> The more comfortable they are, the more their minds open and they flow into
> English effortlessly.

Teaching methods listed:
- Learning through arts and play
- Speaking-focused instruction
- Drawing, building, listening to stories, role-playing
- Singing and music making
- Inventing games
- Humanistic, student-led approach rather than test-focused instruction

Tutor biography (Despina):
- Actor and musician
- Post-secondary studies in sociology
- Graduate of Mayfield Regional Arts School, Toronto
- Performance experience across North & South America, Asia, Southeast Asia, Europe
- Self-taught pianist; composes and records original music
- Teaching English through art and play since 2013
- Award of Excellence in Directing — Sears Festival, Canada

Program offerings: private and semi-private seminars, field trips, events.
